Rules & Guidelines

Please read these rules carefully before registering. Fair play ensures a great experience for everyone!

✓

Registration

Complete the registration form with accurate information. Registration closes on March 10, 2025.

✓

Preliminary Round

Online multiple-choice test with 50 questions. Time limit: 45 minutes. Top 50 participants advance to the finale.

✓

Grand Finale

Offline event with multiple rounds including rapid-fire and buzzer rounds. Location: Mumbai Convention Center.

✓

Conduct

No external help or use of electronic devices during the quiz. Any form of cheating will result in immediate disqualification.

✓

Scoring

Each correct answer awards 2 points. No negative marking in preliminary round. Negative marking applies in finale.

✓

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded immediately after the finale. Winners must present valid ID for prize collection.

✓

Communication

All updates will be sent via email and SMS. Check your inbox regularly for important announcements.

✓

Disputes

Quiz master's decision is final. Any disputes should be raised immediately during the event.

Important: By registering for this competition, you agree to abide by all the rules and guidelines mentioned above. Violation of any rule may result in disqualification.
